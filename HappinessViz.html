Expected Happiness With Dating Pool vs Success In Achieving A Date


This plot shows average success in dating on a 0-1 scale,
with expected happiness with the potential dates on a 1-10
scale. Not only was no correlation found between higher scores
and achieving a date, interestingly the highest score group had
no success at all! This information detracts from the hypothesis
that a positive mindset brings positive results.

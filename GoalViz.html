Goal For Speed Dating vs Success In Achieving A Date


This plot shows average success in dating on a 0-1 scale,
with the subject's goal in participating in the speed dating
on the x axis. Every intention listed shows the same degree
of success except for one: looking for a serious relationship.
This intention boasts double the success of any other, as well
as p < 0.05, indicating a strong and significant statistical
correlation. This heavily supports the hypothesis that concerete
intentions bring about respective results.
